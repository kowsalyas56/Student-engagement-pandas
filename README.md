## Python Visualization(pandas,seaborn,matplotlib) Project – Student Engagement Dashboard

## Project Objective
To analyze student engagement levels by studying their project work, internship participation, IQ scores, communication skills, and academic performance, helping identify factors that influence student success.

## Dataset Used
<a href="https://github.com/kowsalyas56/Student-engagement-pandas/blob/main/college.csv">Data Set</a>

## Questions
- How many projects do students typically complete?
- What percentage of students have internship experience?
- How does communication skill relate to academic performance?
- How is IQ distributed among students?
- What is the overall CGPA distribution?
- Are higher-engaged students also academically strong?<br/>
  Dashboard Interactins:<a href="https://github.com/kowsalyas56/Student-engagement-pandas/blob/main/Screenshot%20(101).png">View Dashboard</a>

## Process
- Imported and cleaned student dataset
- Created visualizations: bar chart, pie chart, line graph, density plot, histogram
- Analyzed relationships between communication skills & academic performance
- Visualized student engagement metrics
- Formatted and arranged dashboard for clarity

## Project Insights
- Most students complete 0–5 projects, with a fairly even distribution.
- 60.4% of students have internship experience, showing good practical exposure.
- Academic scores increase as communication skills improve → positive correlation.
- IQ distribution shows a multi-peak pattern, indicating varied mental capabilities.
- CGPA distribution shows most students fall within the middle-to-high performance range.

  ## Final Conclusion

The Student Engagement Dashboard provides a clear understanding of how student activities, skills, and academic performance are connected. It helps educators identify strengths, engagement levels, and areas where students may need support or additional training.
